Get To Know Me

Hi, I'm Dina. I'm interested in all things creative and tech. I’m currently living in Melbourne, Australia. In my free time, I love to oil paint, cook and swim. I advocate adamantly for diversity in the tech industry and push myself to keep breaking barriers as a young African-Australian, Muslim woman. As a developer well-versed in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python and Ruby, I focus on delivering sustainable and successful results. If you can dream it, I can build it.
